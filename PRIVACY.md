# Privacy Policy

**College Launch collects no data.** The plugin is a set of instruction files (skills and
templates). It has no server, no database, no telemetry, and no analytics. Nothing you do
with it is transmitted to, or visible to, the plugin's maintainers.

## Where your data lives

Everything you enter — onboarding answers, documents, funding details, deadlines — is
stored only in the knowledge base location **you choose** during onboarding: local
Markdown files on your machine by default, or a workspace tool you connect (Confluence,
Notion, Google Drive, SharePoint). You can view, edit, or delete it there at any time.
Deleting that folder or those pages removes everything; the plugin keeps no other copy.

## Processing by Claude

The plugin runs inside Claude. Conversations and documents you share are processed by
Anthropic under the [Anthropic Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy)
and the terms of your Claude account. This plugin does not change what Anthropic
collects or retains.

## Connected tools

If you connect a knowledge base, calendar, or complementary tool (see `CONNECTORS.md`),
your data in that tool is governed by that provider's privacy policy. Connector
credentials are held by your Claude configuration, never by this plugin. Community MCP
servers are third-party code — review them before connecting a student account.

## Data minimization

The skills are written to store sensitive values as labels or last-4 digits only — never
full account numbers, SSNs, or credentials. The repository itself ships zero personal
data: only templates, schemas, and one fully fictional example student.
